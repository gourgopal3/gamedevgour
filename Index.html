About Me

Hi, I'm Gourgopal Sen, a passionate game developer with experience in Unity 3D, C#, and game design. I love creating engaging and immersive experiences for players of all ages.

Hello! I’m a passionate Unity game developer with a strong foundation in 3D modeling using Blender. My journey in game development began with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Applications (BCA), and I am currently advancing my skills with a Master’s degree in Computer Applications (MCA).

With hands-on experience in Unity and Blender, I specialize in creating immersive and engaging gaming experiences. My expertise includes designing dynamic gameplay systems, implementing interactive environments, and crafting visually stunning assets. Whether it’s developing complex character controls or generating endless procedurally generated worlds, I thrive on tackling creative and technical challenges.

Here’s a glimpse of what I do:

  • Game Development: Building interactive and captivating games with Unity, focusing on seamless gameplay and player engagement.
  • 3D Modeling: Crafting detailed and optimized 3D assets using Blender to enhance visual storytelling and game aesthetics.
  • Technical Skills: Proficient in C# programming, Unity3D, Blender3D, and game design principles.

I am always exploring new technologies and methodologies to push the boundaries of game development. Feel free to explore my portfolio and get in touch for collaborations or inquiries.
